What will the assessment look like?
52 marks – 55 minutes long.
You will complete the 'hazardous earth' and 'challenges of an urbanising world' sections of Paper 1
You will have a mixture of question styles including:
  • Short answer questions (2–4 marks)
  • One 8 mark essay question using case studies
  • Multiple choice
  • Graphs / maps / charts analysis
  • Numeracy
